What is WOTD

Originally made popular by Crossfit community, the WOTD or Workout Of The Day is simply a session that revolves around a predetermined number of sets and repetitions of different exercises to be timed for completion. As these workouts are pre-planned by qualified coaches, it takes the technical burden of planning an effective workout off your shoulders so you could enjoy working out stress free.

Is WOTD For Me

WOTD is simply structured so you get to workout one on one with a certified personal trainer on a per session basis without the commitments of any membership packages. Our workouts are ideal if you are on a time constrain or simply curious about how to embark on a healthier lifestyle but not quite ready for the commitment that a gym membership or personal training entails.
